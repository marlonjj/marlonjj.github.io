Marlon Jovez is 23 years old. Born in San Francisco and raised in Pinole, he has been a Bay Area Native all of his life. He attended Catholic private schools through elementary and high school. In high school, he attended Saint Mary's College High School in Berkeley. When he turned 18, he chose to atten San Jose State University (also in the Bay Area) to study Business Marketing. He now attends a Coding Bootcamp through Trilogy at UC Berkeley Extenstion.

Since Marlon was young, he has had an affinity for entertainment. He has been playing video games since he was 3 years old. Taught by his dad, William, Marlon started by using a PC to play Starcraft. At the age of 6, Marlon joined his first choir at his local church. At age 10, Marlon began acting and dancing through school productions and the nearby dance studio. Throughout his middle and high school years, Marlon continued to sing, dance, act, play sports, and play video games every day. Now, Marlon works in the Esports and Online Entertainment industry: managing and ensuring growth for streamers, athletes, and communities. Marlon hopes to work and compete in Esports and Online Entertainment for the rest of his life.
